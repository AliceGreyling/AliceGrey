What is Pact Tactics?

Pact Tactics is a multi-media art piece that centers around how sex affects us as a society and personally explores multiple forms of sexual trauma and experiences.

Multiple Pact Tactics pieces have been featured in "Under Skin, Over Paper", an art show that took place in Detroit in the summer of 2022.
(The Door, Dec. 16th, Home Run, & Something Like This)

Rith of Asariths first official album, "Pact Tactics", which is directly made for the Pact Tactics art experience, is currently in development

Because of the sensitive nature of this art project, all images are hidden behind NSFW warnings with descriptions of what could possibly be triggering.

Physical Art


On Display

On Display is a mixed media piece incorporating photographs with pen drawings.
The idea around this piece is the mental battle of deciding if how your presenting yourself
is really yourself or just a concept you've made to please other people.


Dec. 16th, 2019

This is an installation piece that has multiple media of art
The story behind this installation is a story I wrote in the style of a letter, of a victim confronting their sexual abuser.
Along the writing is drawings and other objects that I have destroyed or altered in a way to add to the story.


Swing and a Miss

Swing and a Miss is black painting with white gel pen drawings.
This painting showcases the feeling of resorting to violence in sexual assult situation.




The Door

The Door is my largest and most extensive piece to date.
The idea behind The Door is to depict a teenager going insane as they're stuck in the closet.
Battling with their sexuality and suicidal thoughts.
