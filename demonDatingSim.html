Story

Welcome to Camp Ghonnabhur'na! The only circle of hell based off of a summer tradition!

Meet multiple demons as you uncover their secrets of why they've ended up in hell, what their life has been, and how to take them on a date.

Participate in the hell games for your one chance to escape to another after life or back to the mortal plain.


Characters

Voltz

Voltz is a badass demon with no tolerance for camp life. They often spend their time alone on the beach, playing music, or reading comic books.

Jolvanika

Jolvanika a hardworking and sporty tomboy that spends most of her time slaving over her camp chores. Though she might be seem married to the job, she might have a trouble maker side to her all along.

Ra'zala

Ra'zala is the new staff member on camp grounds and is the child of The Director. Her hatred for the camp is almost as great as her hatred for you.
