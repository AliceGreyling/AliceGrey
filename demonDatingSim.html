Story

Welcome to Vamp Gonnabhur’na! A summer camp located in the circles of hell!!

In this visual novel dating sim you’ll be playing as a freshly dead soul that has been transferred
to a demon summer camp to live out the rest of your eternity.
Here you’ll meet all kinds of friendly faces (and not-so-friendly ones) as you
solve the mysteries of the camp, play through the hell games, and find some juicy romance
on your journey to find a way out of this hell!

Characters

Voltz

Voltz is a badass demon with no tolerance for camp life. They often spend their time alone on the beach, playing music, or reading comic books.
---

Jolvanika

Jolvanika a hardworking and sporty tomboy that spends most of her time slaving over her camp chores. Though she might be seem married to the job, she might have a trouble maker side to her all along.

Azamith

Azamith is a shy book nerd that enjoys the simple things in life like walking through the woods and looking up at the stars. Though she seems timid, she's as stubborn as she is dorky.
