WHO AM I?

My name is AliceGrey / Rith (they/she)
I'm a Simulation, Animation, and Game Design (SAG) Major at Eastern Michigan University.

I make indie games such as the turn-based RPG “The Bucket List” and the demon dating sim “Camp Ghonnabhur’na”.

painting, pen drawing, digital/pixel art, installation pieces, body art, and music.
